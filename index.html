The Dollars and Miles: NWA Wrestling Territories, Cities, and Stories in the Mid-Late Twentieth Century

Any time the territory system is mentioned in wrestling media a map is shown. These maps have never been good or interactive. This map is an intervention

With the exception of the NWF, the wrestling territories are connected and mutually exclusive, even if sometimes promoters ran shows outside of their territory proper.

Not featured on the map is Canada, which was also part of the NWA system - Wrestling territories in Hawai or Puerto Rico will be added in the future

See the legend in the bottom left corner to turn on off the cities in each territory

Each territory is marked by a primary color used on local TV
